# Pyrogen

Indo-European language construction API

---

## Inputs

- conjugation dictionaries
- morpheme objects

## Outputs

- clauses and sentences with rich language metadata generated from a database of languages
- questions:
  - fill in blank
  - highlight
  - permute & T / F
  - translate

## Principal Components

### Morpheme

- the smallest unit of language
  <br />
- types - root - carries meaning
  - productive - extends meaning
  - connector - dumb / connects other morphemes
    <br />
- inflection - spelling mutation based on tense, case, voice, aspect, person, number, gender, mood
  <br />

### Clause

- 1 verb per clause (requirement)
  - one of ...
    - transitive (has a direct object)
    - intransitive (no direct object)
    - copula (no direct object, definition: to be)

<br />

- verbal noun / verbal adjective
  - does **NOT** count as the verb in a clause
  - infinitive (to run) and gerund (running, /= verbal adj. running, trick - can you substitute "the action of \_\_\_")
  - verbal adjective and verbal noun can take objects

<br />

- dependency
  - subordinating (depends on another clause)
  - coordinating (belongs to a series of non dependent clauses)

<br />

- component structure

  - one of ...

    - subject verb object
    - subject verb
    - subject copula predicate
    - subject copula (existential use of the copula)
    - absolute construction (rare)
      <br />

  - there may be multiple subjects or objects
  - predicate can be noun / reg. adj. / verbal adj. / verbal noun
  - an entire clause can be a predicate
  - subject may be an adj., known as the substantive
